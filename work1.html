Graphic Poster

This is my graphic poster. Susan was waiting for her train on the platform with her friend, Michael. He wanted her to take a half of a sandwich he had left over from dinner. She told him she didn't want it, but he kept insisting through the course of their conversation. Susan and Michael lived in different directions and her train came in first, so she said goodnight to him and got on the train and sat down.
